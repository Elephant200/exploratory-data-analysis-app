About the EDA Chatbot

Your intelligent companion for data exploration and analysis

Welcome to the EDA Chatbot, your powerful tool for exploratory data analysis. This innovative chatbot leverages the cutting-edge capabilities of Google Gemini 2.0 Flash and code execution to transform your data into actionable insights. Simply upload your CSV file and start asking questions - it's that easy!

Whether you're a data scientist, business analyst, or student, our chatbot is designed to help you understand your data better through interactive analysis, visualizations, and natural language conversations.
